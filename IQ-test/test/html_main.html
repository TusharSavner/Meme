Before we begin





1. A robot starts at point (0,0) on a grid and can only move right or up. How many unique paths are there to reach (3,2)?



2. A train leaves Station A at 5:00 PM and travels at 60 km/h. Another train leaves Station B at 5:30 PM towards Station A at 90 km/h. The distance between A and B is 300 km. At what time will they cross paths?



3. Which of the following is the odd one out?




4. You walk into a room with 2 dogs, 4 cats, and a giraffe. How many legs are in the room?



5. If 6 pencils cost the same as 4 pens, and 3 pens cost the same as 2 markers, what is the ratio of the cost of a pencil to a marker?



6. A box has 5 red balls, 4 blue balls, and 3 green balls. If two balls are drawn without replacement, what is the probability both are red?




7. You are in a dark room with a candle, a wood stove, and a gas lamp. You only have one match. What do you light first?



8. A cyclist travels 30 km at 10 km/h and returns the same distance at 15 km/h. What is the average speed for the entire trip?



9. If it takes 5 machines 5 minutes to make 5 widgets, how long would it take 100 machines to make 100 widgets?



10. A man buys a horse for $60, sells it for $70, buys it back for $80, and sells it again for $90. How much profit did he make?
